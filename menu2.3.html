All right, amigos, before we start driving, a quick Spanish 101 lesson that will help you with the road signs along the way. In Spanish, Este = East, Oeste = West, Norte = North, and Sur = South. Great, now we are ready!

  • Take Route #66 East to Route #3 East toward Fajardo.
